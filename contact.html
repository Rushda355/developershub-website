FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How do I get started with a project?

Simply fill out our contact form or book a free discovery call. We will discuss your requirements, goals and timeline and provide a detailed proposal within 2-3 business days.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website takes 2-4 weeks, while a full SaaS platform may take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages after project delivery. Our team remains available for bug fixes, updates and feature additions as your business grows.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, AWS and more. For AI projects we use OpenAI, LangChain, TensorFlow and other leading frameworks.

How much does a project cost?

Pricing depends on project scope, complexity and timeline. We offer transparent, milestone-based pricing with no hidden fees. Book a free call and we will provide a detailed quote tailored to your needs.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients globally across all time zones. Our team uses collaborative tools like Slack, Notion and Zoom to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.
